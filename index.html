About Us

Welcome to Miss Brown's Cafe, nestled in the heart of Napier Ahuriri, where we bring you the delicious charm of homemade roots food with a contemporary twist.

Our passion for crafting exceptional dishes shines through every bite, as we infuse traditional flavors with modern creativity. At Miss Brown's, we take pride in serving Windy Ridge Fair Trade organic Coffee from Ebony Coffee, ensuring each cup is not just a drink but a journey of sustainable and rich flavors.

Join us for a culinary experience that celebrates the essence of local ingredients, thoughtful cooking, and a warm, inviting ambiance. Let your taste buds dance with delight at Miss Brown's Cafe!
